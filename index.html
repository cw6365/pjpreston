About Our Company

P. J. Preston (Auto-Electrical) was formed in its present state in 1978 but its origins can be traced back to January 1956 when it’s founder Paul John Preston formed the company. It’s grown from humble beginnings to a well-known dependable local business and national supplier.

 

What We Do

We hold a large stock of auto-electrical parts, which include; batteries, starters, alternators, reverse camera systems, lighting, cable and much more. Our sourcing of parts, takes us worldwide, with a firm emphasis on quality, pricing and reliability, which in turn benefit our trading partners.
